Tourist Attraction

Pyin Oo Lwin or Pyin U Lwin (Shan: ဝဵင်းပၢင်ႇဢူး, Weng⁴ Paang²uu⁴, "City of Paang²uu⁴"; Burmese: ပြင်ဦးလွင်‌; MLCTS: prang u: lwang, pronounced [pjɪ̀ɰ̃ ʔú lwɪ̀ɰ̃]), formerly and colloquially referred to as Maymyo (Burmese: မေမြို့; MLCTS: me mrui.), is a scenic hill town in the Mandalay Region, Myanmar, some 67 kilometers (42 mi) east of Mandalay, and at an elevation of 1,070 metres (3,510 ft). The town was estimated to have a population of around 255,000 in 2014.

Kan Daw Gyi, Pyin Oo Lwin
